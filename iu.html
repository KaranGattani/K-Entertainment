IU

South Korean Singer-songwriter and Actress

Biography

IU (born Lee Ji-eun on May 16, 1993) is a South Korean singer-songwriter and actress. She debuted in 2008 under LOEN Entertainment. IU is known for her wide vocal range and songwriting skills, making her one of South Korea's most popular solo artists.

Throughout her career, IU has released multiple successful albums and singles, including hits like Good Day and Palette. She has also acted in popular dramas such as Moon Lovers: Scarlet Heart Ryeo and Hotel Del Luna.
