ATEEZ

South Korean Boy Group

Biography

ATEEZ is a South Korean boy band formed by KQ Entertainment. The group debuted on October 24, 2018, with the extended play Treasure EP.1: All to Zero. ATEEZ consists of eight members: Hongjoong, Seonghwa, Yunho, Yeosang, San, Mingi, Wooyoung, and Jongho.

ATEEZ has gained a reputation for their powerful performances, fierce stage presence, and global appeal. They are often referred to as “4th Generation Leaders” and have an extensive international fanbase, especially in the U.S. and Europe. Their music blends hip-hop, EDM, and pop with high-energy choreography and themes of exploration and freedom.
