Synopsis

True Beauty is a South Korean television series based on the Line Webtoon of the same name. It aired from December 2020 to February 2021 and stars Moon Ga-young, Cha Eun-woo, and Hwang In-youp.

The story follows Lim Ju-kyung, a high school girl who uses makeup to hide her real face and boost her confidence. As she navigates school, romance, and friendships, the drama explores themes of self-esteem, identity, and acceptance.
