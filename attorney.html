Extraordinary Attorney Woo

2022 South Korean Drama

Synopsis

Extraordinary Attorney Woo centers around Woo Young-woo, a brilliant attorney with autism spectrum disorder who joins a major law firm. Despite social challenges, her exceptional memory and legal skills help her solve difficult cases and inspire those around her.

The series is praised for its heartfelt portrayal of neurodiversity and legal drama, starring Park Eun-bin in the lead role.
