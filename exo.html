EXO

South Korean-Chinese Boy Band

Biography

EXO is a renowned South Korean-Chinese boy band formed by SM Entertainment in 2012. Originally debuting with 12 members split into two subgroups, EXO-K and EXO-M, the group is known for blending genres like pop, R&B, and EDM with powerful visuals and choreography.

Some of their biggest hits include Growl, Call Me Baby, Love Shot, and Tempo. They are widely recognized for their influence in spreading the Korean Wave globally.
