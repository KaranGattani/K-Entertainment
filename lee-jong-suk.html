Lee Jong-suk

South Korean Actor and Model

Biography

Lee Jong-suk, born on September 14, 1989, is a renowned South Korean actor and former model. He made his breakthrough in School 2013 and later starred in popular dramas like Pinocchio, W, and While You Were Sleeping.

Known for his expressive acting, distinctive visuals, and romantic drama performances, Lee Jong-suk has consistently ranked among the top Hallyu stars with a massive international fanbase.
