Synopsis

Goblin, also known as Guardian: The Lonely and Great God, is a fantasy romance drama that aired from December 2016 to January 2017. It stars Gong Yoo as Kim Shin, a modern-day goblin who needs a human bride to end his immortality. The show also features Lee Dong-wook, Kim Go-eun, and Yoo In-na.

With its poetic narrative, beautiful cinematography, and compelling characters, Goblin became a cultural phenomenon in South Korea and among international K-drama fans.
