Weightlifting Fairy Kim Bok-joo

2016 Sports Romantic Comedy K-Drama

Synopsis

Weightlifting Fairy Kim Bok-joo is a beloved South Korean drama starring Lee Sung-kyung and Nam Joo-hyuk. The story follows Kim Bok-joo, a talented collegiate weightlifter navigating the challenges of youth, love, and friendship.

Combining heartfelt moments with lighthearted humor, the drama highlights themes of dreams, personal growth, and the bonds formed through sports and life.
