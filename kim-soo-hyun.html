Kim Soo-hyun

South Korean Actor

Biography

Kim Soo-hyun, born on February 16, 1988, is one of South Korea's most celebrated actors. He rose to fame with hit dramas like Dream High, Moon Embracing the Sun, and My Love from the Star.

Known for his powerful performances and emotional depth, Kim has become a global Hallyu star. He has won numerous awards and is one of the highest-paid actors in the industry.
