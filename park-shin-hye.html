Park Shin-hye

South Korean Actress and Singer

Biography

Park Shin-hye, born on February 18, 1990, is a prominent South Korean actress and singer. She began her career as a child actor and became a household name with leading roles in dramas like You’re Beautiful, The Heirs, and Pinocchio.

Admired for her versatile acting and warm on-screen presence, Park has also made her mark in film and endorsements. She remains one of Korea’s top actresses with a global fanbase.
