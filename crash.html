Synopsis

Crash Landing on You tells the story of Yoon Se-ri (Son Ye-jin), a wealthy South Korean heiress and businesswoman, who crash-lands in North Korea while paragliding. There, she meets Ri Jeong-hyeok (Hyun Bin), a stoic North Korean army officer who helps her hide and eventually attempts to return her to the South.

With its emotional depth, cross-border romance, and stunning performances, the series became a massive international success, contributing significantly to the global popularity of K-dramas.
