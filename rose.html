Rosé

Member of BLACKPINK, South Korean Singer & Dancer

Biography

Rosé, born Roseanne Park on February 11, 1997, in New Zealand and raised in Australia, is a South Korean singer and dancer. She is a member of the globally renowned K-pop girl group BLACKPINK under YG Entertainment.

Known for her unique vocal tone and impressive stage presence, Rosé debuted with BLACKPINK in 2016 and has since also launched a successful solo career.
