Song Hye-kyo

South Korean Actress

Biography

Song Hye-kyo, born on November 22, 1981, is a highly acclaimed South Korean actress. She gained widespread fame through her roles in classic K-dramas like Autumn in My Heart, Full House, and the global hit Descendants of the Sun.

Known for her elegance and emotional depth, Song Hye-kyo has become a symbol of the Korean Wave (Hallyu), and continues to receive critical acclaim for both her television and film work.
