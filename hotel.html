Hotel Del Luna

2019 Fantasy Romance Drama

Synopsis

Hotel Del Luna is a 2019 South Korean fantasy romance television series starring IU and Yeo Jin-goo. The drama follows Jang Man-wol, the beautiful but moody owner of a mystical hotel that caters exclusively to ghosts.

Cursed due to a sin she committed a millennium ago, Man-wol is bound to the hotel until she can find peace. Her life takes a turn when Gu Chan-sung, a young hotelier, is forced to manage the hotel and help its guests find closure. The series blends emotional stories, haunting visuals, and romantic tension into a captivating supernatural tale.
