Synopsis

Healer is a South Korean television drama that aired from 2014 to 2015, starring Ji Chang-wook, Park Min-young, and Yoo Ji-tae. The story revolves around a night courier known only as "Healer," whose real name is Seo Jung-hoo. His life takes a dramatic turn when he crosses paths with Chae Young-shin, a reporter, and Kim Moon-ho, a star journalist with a hidden past.

Blending action, romance, and mystery, Healer uncovers a decades-old conspiracy involving a group of five friends from the 1980s. With intense chemistry between the leads, stylish fight choreography, and a gripping storyline, the drama has earned a cult following among K-drama fans.
