NMIXX

South Korean Girl Group

Biography

NMIXX is a South Korean girl group formed by JYP Entertainment. They officially debuted on February 22, 2022, with the single album Ad Mare. The group originally consisted of seven members: Lily, Haewon, Sullyoon, Jinni, Bae, Jiwoo, and Kyujin. Following Jinni’s departure in December 2022, NMIXX now promotes as a six-member group.

Known for their experimental and genre-blending music style referred to as “MIXX POP,” NMIXX has carved a unique niche in the K-pop industry. Their strong vocal performances, intricate choreography, and bold visual concepts have drawn attention from fans worldwide.
