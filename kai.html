Kai

South Korean Singer, Dancer, EXO & SuperM Member

Biography

Kim Jong-in, known professionally as Kai, was born on January 14, 1994. He is a South Korean singer, dancer, model, and actor, best known as a member of the K-pop boy group EXO, its sub-unit EXO-K, and the supergroup SuperM.

Renowned for his charismatic performances and stunning dance skills, Kai has earned the title of one of the best dancers in K-pop.
