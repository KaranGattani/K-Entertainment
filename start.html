Start-Up

2020 South Korean Drama

Synopsis

Start-Up tells the story of young entrepreneurs aspiring to launch virtual dreams into reality in South Korea’s high-tech industry. It stars Bae Suzy, Nam Joo-hyuk, Kim Seon-ho, and Kang Han-na, weaving themes of ambition, love, and friendship.

The drama highlights the trials of building a start-up company, family ties, and finding one’s true path amid challenges and romance.
