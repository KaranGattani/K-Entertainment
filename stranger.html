Stranger (Secret Forest)

2017 Crime Thriller K-Drama

Synopsis

Stranger, also known as Secret Forest, is a critically acclaimed South Korean crime thriller television series starring Cho Seung-woo and Bae Doona. The series follows a prosecutor who, due to a brain surgery that reduced his emotions, teams up with a passionate detective to uncover corruption and solve mysterious murder cases.

Praised for its tight plotting, strong performances, and dark atmosphere, Stranger dives deep into themes of justice, power, and moral ambiguity in the Korean judicial system.
