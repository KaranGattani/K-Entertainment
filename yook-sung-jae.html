Yook Sung-jae

South Korean Singer and Actor

Biography

Yook Sung-jae, born on May 2, 1995, is a South Korean singer, actor, and member of the boy group BTOB. He gained popularity as an actor through roles in Who Are You: School 2015 and the hit fantasy drama Goblin.

Known for his vocal talent, charm, and versatility, Yook has built a strong presence both on stage and screen, establishing himself as a respected K-pop idol and actor.
