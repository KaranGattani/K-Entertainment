Park Min-young

South Korean Actress

Biography

Park Min-young, born on March 4, 1986, is a beloved South Korean actress known for her versatility in romantic comedies and dramas. She gained popularity through series like City Hunter, Healer, What's Wrong with Secretary Kim, and Her Private Life.

Known for her charm, emotional range, and comedic timing, Park Min-young has established herself as a leading actress in Hallyu.
