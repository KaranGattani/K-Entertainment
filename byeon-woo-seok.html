Byeon Woo-seok

South Korean Actor and Model

Biography

Byeon Woo-seok, born on October 31, 1991, is a South Korean actor and model gaining recognition for his rising career. He starred in dramas such as Record of Youth, Moonshine, and most recently Lovely Runner, which brought him international fame.

His tall physique and expressive acting have made him a sought-after lead in romantic and youth-centric dramas.
