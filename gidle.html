(G)I-DLE

South Korean Girl Group

Biography

(G)I-DLE is a South Korean girl group formed by Cube Entertainment. The group debuted on May 2, 2018, and quickly rose to popularity with their debut single LATATA. The group originally consisted of six members: Miyeon, Minnie, Soojin, Soyeon, Yuqi, and Shuhua.

Known for their creative autonomy and involvement in music production, especially by leader Soyeon, (G)I-DLE has released numerous hit songs including HANN (Alone), Oh My God, TOMBOY, and NXDE. The group has been praised for their bold concepts, artistry, and stage presence.
