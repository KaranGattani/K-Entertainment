Nayeon (Im Na-yeon)

Member of TWICE, South Korean Singer

Biography

Im Na-yeon, known mononymously as Nayeon, was born on September 22, 1995. She is a South Korean singer and the oldest member of the girl group TWICE, formed by JYP Entertainment through the reality show Sixteen in 2015.

Nayeon gained massive popularity for her vibrant personality, strong vocals, and charming visuals. In 2022, she debuted as a solo artist with her mini-album IM NAYEON, making her the first member of TWICE to go solo. The album was a commercial success both domestically and internationally.
