ITZY

South Korean Girl Group

Biography

ITZY is a South Korean girl group formed by JYP Entertainment, debuting in 2019 with the hit single Dalla Dalla. The group consists of five members: Yeji, Lia, Ryujin, Chaeryeong, and Yuna.

Known for their energetic performances and empowering messages, ITZY quickly rose to fame with hits like Wannabe, Not Shy, and LOCO. They are recognized for their confident style and catchy music.
