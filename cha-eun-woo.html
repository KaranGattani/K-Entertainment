Cha Eun-woo

South Korean Singer, Actor & Model

Biography

Cha Eun-woo, born Lee Dong-min on March 30, 1997, is a South Korean singer, actor, and model. He debuted as a member of the boy group Astro in 2016 under Fantagio. Known for his striking visuals, he gained massive popularity through his acting roles in dramas such as My ID is Gangnam Beauty and True Beauty.

Aside from his acting and music career, Cha Eun-woo has been active in various variety shows and has received numerous awards for his performances and visuals. He continues to be one of the most sought-after Hallyu stars of his generation.
