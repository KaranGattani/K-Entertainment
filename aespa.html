aespa

South Korean Girl Group

Biography

aespa is a South Korean girl group formed by SM Entertainment. They debuted on November 17, 2020, with the single Black Mamba. The group consists of four members: Karina, Giselle, Winter, and Ningning.

Known for their futuristic concept involving virtual avatars, aespa has made a strong impact with songs like Next Level, Savage, and Girls. Their innovative approach and powerful performances have earned them a strong international following.
