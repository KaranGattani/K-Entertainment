NCT

South Korean Boy Group with Unlimited Members

Biography

NCT (Neo Culture Technology) is a South Korean boy group formed by SM Entertainment with a unique concept of having unlimited members divided into multiple sub-units. Debuted in 2016, NCT includes units such as NCT 127, NCT Dream, and WayV (Chinese sub-unit).

Known for their diverse music styles, powerful performances, and multi-national lineup, NCT has released hits like Kick It, Cherry Bomb, Make A Wish, and Dream Launch. The group pushes boundaries with innovative music and concepts.
