BTS

South Korean Boy Band

Biography

BTS, also known as Bangtan Boys, is a South Korean boy band formed by Big Hit Entertainment. The group debuted in 2013 and consists of RM, Jin, SUGA, j-hope, Jimin, V, and Jungkook. Renowned for their meaningful lyrics, diverse musical styles, and impressive performances, BTS has become one of the most influential musical acts globally.

BTS has broken numerous records, topped global charts, and sparked a worldwide fanbase known as ARMY. Beyond music, the group has made significant cultural impact through philanthropy and advocacy, including their partnership with UNICEF for the "Love Myself" campaign.
