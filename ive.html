IVE

South Korean Girl Group

Biography

IVE is a South Korean girl group formed by Starship Entertainment. They debuted on December 1, 2021, with the single album Eleven. The group consists of six members: Yujin, Gaeul, Rei, Wonyoung, Liz, and Leeseo.

Known for their confident and elegant image, IVE quickly gained popularity with hit songs like Love Dive, After LIKE, and I AM. Their success has cemented them as one of the leading fourth-generation K-pop girl groups, receiving numerous awards and topping charts both domestically and internationally.
