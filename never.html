Nevertheless

2021 South Korean Drama

Synopsis

Nevertheless follows the story of Yoo Na-bi, a hopeless romantic who has lost faith in love, and Park Jae-eon, a charming art student who is reluctant to commit. The series explores their complicated relationship filled with attraction, misunderstandings, and emotional growth.

Starring Han So-hee and Song Kang, the drama offers a realistic take on modern romance and the dilemmas young adults face in love.
