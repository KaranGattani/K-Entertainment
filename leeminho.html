Lee Min-ho

South Korean Actor & Model

Biography

Lee Min-ho, born on June 22, 1987, is a prominent South Korean actor, singer, and model. He rose to fame through his iconic role as Gu Jun-pyo in the hit drama Boys Over Flowers (2009), and has since starred in numerous successful series such as The Heirs, Legend of the Blue Sea, and The King: Eternal Monarch.

Known for his tall frame, charming looks, and charismatic performances, Lee Min-ho is one of the most influential Hallyu stars globally.
