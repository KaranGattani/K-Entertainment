Vincenzo

2021 South Korean Crime-Drama Series

Synopsis

Vincenzo is a 2021 South Korean drama starring Song Joong-ki as Vincenzo Cassano, a Korean-Italian mafia consigliere who returns to South Korea after a betrayal in Italy. With sharp wit and a cold demeanor, he delivers his own brand of justice while forming unlikely bonds with residents of a quirky building.

The series masterfully blends dark comedy, legal drama, and action, receiving international acclaim for its writing, stylish cinematography, and charismatic performances.
