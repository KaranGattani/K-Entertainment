Reply 1988

2015 Slice-of-Life K-Drama

Synopsis

Reply 1988 is a critically acclaimed South Korean television drama that aired in 2015. It is the third installment in the beloved “Reply” series, following Reply 1997 and Reply 1994. The drama is set in the year 1988 in the Ssangmun-dong neighborhood of Seoul and follows the lives of five childhood friends and their families.

Combining heartfelt moments with humor and nostalgia, Reply 1988 beautifully portrays themes of friendship, family, first love, and growing up. The series features an ensemble cast including Hyeri, Ryu Jun-yeol, Park Bo-gum, Go Kyung-pyo, and Lee Dong-hwi.
