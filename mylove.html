My Love from the Star

2013 Sci-fi Romance K-Drama

Synopsis

My Love from the Star is a 2013 South Korean romantic fantasy drama starring Kim Soo-hyun and Jun Ji-hyun. The series tells the story of Do Min-joon, an alien who landed on Earth 400 years ago during the Joseon Dynasty and falls in love with a famous actress in modern-day Seoul, Cheon Song-yi.

With his near-perfect appearance, intelligence, and superpowers, Do Min-joon has lived a quiet life until Cheon Song-yi disrupts his world. The drama blends science fiction, romance, comedy, and mystery, becoming a massive hit across Asia and solidifying the Hallyu wave.
