SEVENTEEN

Self-Producing South Korean Boy Group

Biography

SEVENTEEN is a South Korean boy group formed by Pledis Entertainment in 2015. Consisting of 13 members, the group is divided into three sub-units: Hip-Hop, Vocal, and Performance. Known for their synchronized choreography and self-produced albums, SEVENTEEN has earned the nickname “self-producing idols.”

Their notable songs include Don't Wanna Cry, Very Nice, Hot, and Super. The group is known for their creativity, unity, and powerful stage performances.
