Karina

South Korean Singer, Dancer, and Member of aespa

Biography

Karina, born Yoo Ji-min on April 11, 2000, is a South Korean singer, dancer, and leader of the K-pop girl group aespa under SM Entertainment. She debuted with aespa in November 2020 with the single Black Mamba.

Known for her powerful stage presence and futuristic visuals, Karina quickly rose to fame as one of the most iconic fourth-generation K-pop idols. She is also a member of the project supergroup GOT the Beat under SM's Girls On Top initiative.
