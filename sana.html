Sana (Minatozaki Sana)

Member of TWICE, Japanese Singer and K-pop Idol

Biography

Minatozaki Sana, born on December 29, 1996, in Osaka, Japan, is a Japanese singer based in South Korea. She rose to fame as one of the most popular members of the girl group TWICE, formed by JYP Entertainment through the reality show Sixteen in 2015.

Known for her bright personality, adorable expressions, and strong stage presence, Sana has captured hearts across Asia. Her iconic “shy shy shy” line in the song Cheer Up contributed to TWICE’s rise in popularity and became a viral sensation.
