Hwang Min-hyun

South Korean Singer and Actor

Biography

Hwang Min-hyun, born on August 9, 1995, is a South Korean singer and actor. He debuted as a member of the boy group NU'EST in 2012 and later became part of the popular temporary group Wanna One through the show Produce 101 Season 2.

Aside from his music career, Min-hyun has established himself as a talented actor, starring in notable K-dramas such as Alchemy of Souls and Live On. His charismatic presence and vocal talent have earned him a strong fanbase in both music and drama scenes.
