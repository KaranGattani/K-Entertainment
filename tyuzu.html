Tzuyu (Chou Tzuyu)

Member of TWICE, Taiwanese Singer and K-pop Idol

Biography

Chou Tzuyu, born on June 14, 1999, in Tainan, Taiwan, is a Taiwanese singer and the youngest member (maknae) of the South Korean girl group TWICE. She was scouted by JYP Entertainment in Taiwan and moved to South Korea to train, eventually debuting through the survival show Sixteen in 2015.

Tzuyu is widely admired for her stunning visuals, elegance, and calm demeanor. She has become one of the most recognized foreign K-pop idols and enjoys immense popularity in both South Korea and Taiwan.
