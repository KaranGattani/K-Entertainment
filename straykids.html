Stray Kids

High-Energy K-pop Group from JYP Entertainment

Biography

Stray Kids is a South Korean boy group formed by JYP Entertainment through a survival program in 2017. The group consists of 8 members: Bang Chan, Lee Know, Changbin, Hyunjin, Han, Felix, Seungmin, and I.N. They are known for their intense music, self-written lyrics, and explosive performances.

Their hit songs include God's Menu, Back Door, Thunderous, and Maniac. Stray Kids is often praised for their bold sound, genre-blending style, and artistic individuality.
