Baekhyun

South Korean Singer, EXO & SuperM Member

Biography

Byun Baek-hyun, born on May 6, 1992, is a South Korean singer, songwriter, and actor. He is best known as a main vocalist of the immensely popular boy group EXO and its sub-unit EXO-CBX, as well as a member of SuperM, a K-pop supergroup formed by SM Entertainment.

Baekhyun is renowned for his powerful vocals, charisma, and stage presence. He has also had a successful solo career, with chart-topping albums like City Lights, Delight, and Bambi. His influence in the K-pop industry extends beyond group activities, making him one of the most celebrated vocalists of his generation.
