The King: Eternal Monarch

2020 Fantasy Romance K-Drama

Synopsis

The King: Eternal Monarch is a South Korean television series that aired in 2020, starring Lee Min-ho and Kim Go-eun. The drama follows Emperor Lee Gon, who discovers a parallel world where the Republic of Korea exists. To protect his kingdom and those he loves, he must confront dark forces crossing between the two worlds.

Combining fantasy, romance, and political intrigue, the series explores themes of destiny, love, and the battle between good and evil across parallel universes. With its high production value and star-studded cast, the show captivated viewers worldwide.
