Winter

South Korean Singer and Member of aespa

Biography

Winter, born Kim Min-jeong on January 1, 2001, is a South Korean singer and a key member of the girl group aespa under SM Entertainment. She officially debuted with aespa in 2020 with the digital single Black Mamba.

Renowned for her crystal-clear vocals and charming visuals, Winter has quickly emerged as one of the standout fourth-generation K-pop idols. She is also part of SM Entertainment's project group GOT the Beat, showcasing her versatile talent across genres and stages.
