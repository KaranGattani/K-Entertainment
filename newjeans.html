NewJeans

South Korean Girl Group

Biography

NewJeans is a South Korean girl group formed by ADOR, a subsidiary of HYBE Corporation. They officially debuted on July 22, 2022, with the extended play New Jeans. The group consists of five members: Minji, Hanni, Danielle, Haerin, and Hyein.

Known for their refreshing Y2K aesthetic, experimental R&B-pop sound, and minimalist approach, NewJeans made an instant impact in the K-pop scene with hits like Attention, Hype Boy, and Ditto. Their unique concept and non-traditional debut rollout have been praised as innovative, earning them numerous rookie awards and critical acclaim.
