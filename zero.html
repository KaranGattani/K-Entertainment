ZEROBASEONE

South Korean Boy Group

Biography

ZEROBASEONE, also known as ZB1, is a South Korean boy group formed through the Mnet survival show Boys Planet in 2023. Managed by WAKEONE Entertainment, the group consists of nine members: Sung Hanbin, Zhang Hao, Seok Matthew, Kim Taerae, Ricky, Kim Gyuvin, Park Gunwook, Han Yujin, and Kim Jiwoong.

They officially debuted with the mini-album YOUTH IN THE SHADE, instantly making waves with their visuals, vocals, and dynamic performances. Despite being a temporary group, their impact on the 4th generation of K-pop has been massive.
