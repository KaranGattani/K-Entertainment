Lisa

Member of BLACKPINK, South Korean Rapper & Dancer

Biography

Lisa, born Lalisa Manoban on March 27, 1997, in Thailand, is a South Korean rapper, singer, and dancer. She is the main dancer and lead rapper of the internationally acclaimed K-pop girl group BLACKPINK under YG Entertainment.

Known for her charismatic stage presence and exceptional dance skills, Lisa debuted with BLACKPINK in 2016 and quickly became one of the group's standout performers.
