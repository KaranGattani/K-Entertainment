While You Were Sleeping

2017 Fantasy Romance K-Drama

Synopsis

While You Were Sleeping is a 2017 South Korean television series starring Lee Jong-suk, Bae Suzy, and Jung Hae-in. The drama tells the story of a woman who can see future events in her dreams and a prosecutor who tries to prevent those events from happening.

Combining fantasy, romance, and legal drama, the series explores fate, destiny, and the power of changing the future through love and courage. It received praise for its unique storyline and strong performances.
