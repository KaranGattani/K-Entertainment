LE SSERAFIM

South Korean Girl Group

Biography

LE SSERAFIM is a South Korean girl group formed by Source Music and HYBE Corporation. The group debuted on May 2, 2022, with the extended play Fearless. LE SSERAFIM currently consists of five members: Kim Chaewon, Sakura, Huh Yunjin, Kazuha, and Hong Eunchae.

The name LE SSERAFIM is an anagram of "I'm Fearless," symbolizing the group's confident and bold concept. They quickly garnered attention with their distinctive sound, fashion, and empowering lyrics, making waves globally among the fourth-generation K-pop scene.
