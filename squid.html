Squid Game

2021 Survival Thriller Drama

Synopsis

Squid Game is a South Korean survival drama television series created by Hwang Dong-hyuk. Released on Netflix in 2021, it became an international phenomenon for its gripping story and social commentary.

The series follows Seong Gi-hun and hundreds of cash-strapped players who accept a strange invitation to compete in children’s games for a tempting prize — but the stakes are deadly. Squid Game is known for its brutal critique of capitalism, inequality, and human desperation.
