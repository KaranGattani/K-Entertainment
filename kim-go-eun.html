Kim Go-eun

South Korean Actress

Biography

Kim Go-eun, born on July 2, 1991, is a critically acclaimed South Korean actress. She made a powerful debut in the film A Muse (2012) and gained wider popularity through dramas like Cheese in the Trap, Goblin, and The King: Eternal Monarch.

Known for her expressive acting and unique charm, Kim Go-eun has become one of the most respected actresses in the Korean entertainment industry.
