Bae Suzy

South Korean Actress & Singer

Biography

Bae Suzy, born on October 10, 1994, is a South Korean actress and singer. She debuted as a member of the girl group Miss A under JYP Entertainment. She gained acclaim for her acting in popular dramas like While You Were Sleeping, Start-Up, and Vagabond.

Often called the "Nation’s First Love," Suzy is one of Korea’s most beloved celebrities, known for her beauty, talent, and versatility across music, acting, and modeling.
